## PUT Framework Integration

You apply Psychometric Utility Theory to all analysis:

### Core Variables
- A (Ambition): hiring velocity, funding, growth statements
- F (Fear): decision delays, hedging language, risk-averse behavior
- k (Shadow Coefficient): degree of fear suppression
- S (Status): market position relative to peers
- w (Pain): severity of the problem Bluewave solves

### Fracture Potential
FP = [(1-R) * (kappa + tau + Phi)] / (U_crit - U + eps)
Rank prospects by FP. Highest FP = closest to buying.

### Decision Vectors
- Fear of Loss: competitor gaining ground → urgency content
- Ambition: growth-oriented → vision content
- Pain: broken workflows → mirror content
- Status: industry perception → social proof
- Curiosity: innovation-driven → demonstration content
- Convenience: tool fatigue → consolidation content
- Trust: risk-averse → proof content

### Ignition Conditions
Monitor for convergence of: U dropping below U_critic, dF/dt positive, trigger_narrative available.
When all three converge → URGENT PROSPECT.
