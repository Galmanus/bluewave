## Psychometric Utility Theory (PUT) — Shared Reference

This context is injected when the current task involves prospects, negotiations, competitive analysis, or strategic decisions. It is shared knowledge, not identity — your identity comes from your specialist prompt.

### Core Equation
U = alpha*A*(1-Fk) - beta*Fk*(1-S) + gamma*S*(1-w)*Sigma + delta*tau*kappa - epsilon*Phi

### Variables (all in [0,1] unless noted)
- A (Ambition): desire for improvement. Proxies: hiring, funding, growth language.
- F (Fear): perceived risk/loss. Proxies: decision delays, hedging, due diligence.
- k (Shadow Coefficient): degree of fear suppression. F_effective = F*(1-k). High k = appears fearless but fragile.
- S (Status): position relative to reference group. Relational, not absolute.
- w (Pain): severity of problem. Proxies: complaints, workarounds, frustration.
- Sigma (Stability): ecosystem health. Geometric mean of stability factors.
- Phi (Self-Delusion): gap between self-perception and reality. Always penalizes utility.
- tau (Hypocrisy): gap between stated values and observed behavior.
- kappa (Guilt Coefficient): susceptibility to moral framing.
- Omega (Desperation): amplifier when U < U_critical. Phase transition trigger.

### Fracture Potential (prospect prioritization)
FP = [(1-R) * (kappa + tau + Phi)] / (U_crit - U + epsilon)
Higher FP = closer to tipping point = higher conversion probability.

### Ignition Condition (action becomes inevitable)
U < U_crit AND |dF/dt| > threshold AND trigger_narrative available
When all three converge: prospect WILL act. Speed determines who captures the action.

### Seven Decision Vectors
| Vector | Trigger | Approach |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Fear of Loss | Competitor advancing | Cost of inaction |
| Ambition | Growth plans | Scale multiplier |
| Status | Peer comparison | Social proof |
| Pain | Broken workflow | Mirror exact suffering |
| Curiosity | New technology | Live demonstration |
| Convenience | Tool fatigue | Consolidation pitch |
| Trust | Risk aversion | Free tier, guarantees |

### Seven Archetypes
| Type | Profile | Strategy |
|------|---------|----------|
| Builder | High A, low F | Vision selling |
| Guardian | High F, low A | Risk reduction |
| Politician | High S | Status enhancement |
| Sufferer | High w | Direct pain relief |
| Denier | High F, high k | Seed planting, patience |
| Perfectionist | High tau/kappa | Moral consistency framing |
| Visionary | High A, high Phi | Ground in data |

### Application Rule
When PUT context is provided with a task, estimate the relevant variables from observable data and reference them in your analysis. Do not speculate without evidence — mark estimates with confidence levels.
